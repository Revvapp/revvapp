Privacy Policy

Effective May 20, 2026 · Last updated May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Revv (“Revv,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information when you sign up: email address, password (stored in hashed form by Firebase Authentication), full name, phone number, city, and state.
  • Detailer profile information (if you join as a professional detailer): business name, services offered, rates, working days and hours, service area, daily income goal, ID verification status, and any portfolio or profile photos you upload.
  • Booking information: vehicles you save, services you select, scheduled dates and times, addresses where service is performed, and any notes you provide.
  • Vehicle Inspection Report (VIR) photos taken by the detailer before service begins, and before/after photos taken at job completion.
  • Invoices generated when a job completes, including the agreed price, platform fee, and detailer payout.

Information collected automatically

  • Push notification token, a unique identifier tied to your device that lets us deliver notifications about your bookings. We do not use it for tracking across apps or for advertising.
  • Approximate location, only with your permission, used to show detailers near you. We do not collect location data in the background.
  • Crash and error diagnostics sent to our error-monitoring provider (Sentry) when the App encounters an unexpected error. Reports include device model, operating system version, and the technical stack trace of the error. We do not include personal identifiers in error reports.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match clients with nearby professional detailers
  • Process bookings, generate invoices, and track job progress
  • Send transactional push notifications (for example, booking accepted, job started, payment released)
  • Diagnose crashes and improve App reliability
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent fraud and abuse

3. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to operate the service:

  • Between matched users. When a client books a detailer, both parties can see the other’s name, the client’s city, vehicle details, the agreed service and price, and their booking history with each other.
  • With our infrastructure providers. We use Google’s Firebase platform for authentication, the Firestore database, and file storage; Sentry for crash reporting; and Expo for push notification delivery. These providers process information on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy commitments.
  • With payment processors. When payments are enabled, Stripe processes card information directly. We do not store full card numbers on our systems.
  • As required by law. We may disclose information in response to valid legal process, or to protect rights, safety, or property.

We do not sell personal information.

4. Data Retention

We retain account and booking information for as long as your account is active and as needed to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You can request deletion of your account by contacting us at the email below; we will delete your personal information within a reasonable period, subject to any obligations to retain records (for example, tax or transaction records).

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Receive a portable copy of your data in a common format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

All traffic between the App and our backend is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. We use Firebase Authentication, Firestore security rules, and Firebase Storage rules to restrict access to personal data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Users

Revv is operated from the United States. If you use the App from outside the United States, you understand that your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the App or by email. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
