This Privacy Policy explains how Revv ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and
shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using
the App, you agree to the practices described here.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide
- **Account information** when you sign up: email address, password (stored hashed by Firebase Authentication), full name, phone number, city, and state.
- **Detailer profile information** (if you join as a detailer): business name, services offered, rates, working days and hours, service area, daily income goal, ID verification status, and any portfolio photos you upload.
- **Bookings**: vehicles, service selections, scheduled date and time, notes, and the addresses where service will be performed.
- **Vehicle Inspection Report (VIR) photos** taken by the detailer before service, and **before/after photos** taken at job completion.
- **Invoices** generated when a job completes, including the price, platform fee, and payout amount.

### Information collected automatically
- **Device information** when you sign in: an Expo push notification token tied to your device, used to deliver notifications about your bookings.
- **Approximate location** (with your permission), used to find detailers near you. We do not track your location in the background.
- **Crash and error diagnostics** sent to Sentry when the app encounters an error, including device model, OS version, and a stack trace. We do not include personally identifying information in error reports.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information above to:
- Create and manage your account
- Match clients with nearby detailers
- Process bookings, generate invoices, and track job status
- Send transactional push notifications (e.g., booking accepted, payment released)
- Diagnose crashes and improve app reliability
- Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent fraud

## 3. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to operate the service:
- **Between matched users**: when a client books a detailer, both parties can see the other's name, location at the city level, vehicle details, and booking history with each other.
- **With Firebase (Google)** as our infrastructure provider — authentication, database, file storage.
- **With Sentry** for crash reporting.
- **With Stripe** for payment processing (when payments are enabled).
- **With Expo** for push notification delivery.
- **As required by law**: in response to legal process, or to protect rights, safety, or property.

We do **not** sell personal information.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain account and booking information for as long as your account is active and as needed to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You can request deletion of your account by contacting us at the email below.
